School of Engineering & Technology

The Department of Information Technology is a flagship department under the School of Engineering and Technology. Having an up-to-date curriculum, well trained faculty and an excellent infrastructure, the Department is committed to develop highly skilled Information Technology (IT) professionals, capable of meeting the ever-changing and increasing needs of the IT industry. The faculty has a very good profile with a number of publications and patents. Presently existing only as a teaching department, the University has plans to make to it a teaching-cum-research department in near future.

The Department is presently offering a 2 year Post-Graduate programmed leading to the degree of M. Tech. in Information Technology in addition to B. Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) which is a 4 year degree programme. Striving hard for excellence, the Department plans to diversify and expand its activities in the years to come.

The school of Engineering and Technology has the support of ITSS , well equipped labs for the same .

B.Tech CSE Programme

 The Department is presently offering a B. Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) which is a 4 year degree programme. Striving hard for excellence, the Department plans to diversify and expand its activities in the years to come .

The programme is aimed at fulfilling the ever increasing demand of trained computer science professionals in the industry. The students in this course receive rigorous training to face the challenges of today’s competitive world. The course is updated from time to time to keep up with the ever evolving IT sector. Emphasis is laid not only on the theoretical concepts but also on practical experience and industry interaction.
